### 🧠 Master’s Thesis (Ongoing)  
**Limits of Embedding-Based Topic Models**

**Focus:**  
Evaluation of **BERTopic and sentence embeddings** on dense theoretical texts (Marx, Spengler, Weber, Freud)

**Key Idea:**  
Not all data can be cleanly structured — this project investigates **when NLP models fail and why**

**Highlights:**
- Analysis of **semantic structure in embedding space**
- Comparison of **author-written vs. editor-mediated texts**
- Evaluation using:
  - cosine similarity distributions  
  - cluster stability  
  - topic separability  

👉 Demonstrates **critical model evaluation**, not just application  

---

### 🤖 From Headlines to Insights  
**Domain-Specific NLP for Financial News Classification**

**Objective:**  
Develop a **domain-adapted NLP model** to classify crude oil market headlines

**Approach:**
- Fine-tuning transformer models (BERT / RoBERTa)
- Semi-supervised learning on financial text data
- Comparison with rule-based baseline

**Impact:**
- Improved classification accuracy  
- Better detection of **market-relevant signals (supply, demand, geopolitics)**  

👉 Transforms unstructured text → structured market intelligence

### 🛢️ Topic Identification in Oil Market News  
**Structured Classification of Financial Headlines**

**Objective:**  
Identify and structure key drivers in crude oil news flow

**Approach:**
- Development of an **18-class taxonomy**
- Rule-based and keyword-based classification
- Iterative refinement of topic structure

**Outcome:**
- Clear separation of **price vs. structural market drivers**
- Foundation for automated **news monitoring systems**

### 📊 Crypto Listing Events Dashboard  
**Event-Driven Price Analysis**

**Objective:**  
Analyze how listing announcements on crypto exchanges affect price behavior

**Features:**
- Time-aligned price analysis (t = 0 after listing)
- Topic-based grouping of events
- Interactive filtering and comparison

**Insights:**
- Listing events trigger **short-term volatility patterns**
- Potential for **event-driven trading strategies**

👉 Combines data engineering, visualization, and financial reasoning  

---

### 🌍 Air Quality & Weather Analytics Dashboard  
**Long-Term Time Series Analysis (R / Shiny)**

**Objective:**  
Analyze relationships between air pollution (PM10) and weather conditions

**Features:**
- STL decomposition (trend, seasonality, residual)
- Pixel heatmaps for long-term patterns
- Interactive outlier detection
- Multi-variable comparison

**Insights:**
- Clear **downward pollution trend**
- Strong **seasonal effects**
- Evidence of **rain reducing pollution (“wash-out effect”)**

👉 Demonstrates strong **analytical thinking + visual analytics**
